THE SCHOOL IS FULL OF FACILITIES

The program was developed in accordance with the Decree of the President of September 5, 2018 No. PF-5538 "On additional measures to improve the public education management system".!

By June 1, 2019, it is planned to develop and approve model projects of modern general education institutions based on international experience, taking into account the introduction of advanced technologies and engineering solutions. At the same time, new regulations on equipping educational institutions with the necessary furniture, modern educational and laboratory equipment, textbooks and educational materials, computer and multimedia equipment, and video surveillance systems were approved.
